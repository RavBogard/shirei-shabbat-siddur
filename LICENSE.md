## 4 · The Mi Shebeirach setting — NOT included

At Central Reform Congregation, the prayer for healing is sung to the beloved
**Debbie Friedman / Drorah Setel "Mi Shebeirach" (1988)**, which is **under copyright
and is not included in this repository**. The committed file
`build/typst/content/mi-shebeirach-placeholder.typ` holds a public-domain-safe
rendering of the classical healing formula in its place. Each community adapting
this book must either **license the Friedman/Setel setting from its rights holders**
or **substitute its own healing prayer** in that unit. See `FORK-YOUR-OWN.md`
("The Mi Shebeirach placeholder") for the mechanics.
