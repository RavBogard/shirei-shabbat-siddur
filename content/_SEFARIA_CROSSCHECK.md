# Sefaria Cross-Check — Migrated Draft (Phase 0)

Generated 2026-07-03 during the typesetting spike. Every unit's Hebrew was compared
against Sefaria via the MCP. **Nothing was silently changed** — the content files
reproduce your June 19 draft exactly. This is the flag list for your review.

## Global (all units)

- **Divine name spelling.** Your draft writes the Name as **יְהוָה** (yud-sh'va, heh,
  vav-kamatz — no *cholam* over the heh). Sefaria's Masoretic text writes **יְהֹוָה**
  (with the *cholam*). Your style guide says to keep יְהוָה "as in the draft," so this is
  a **deliberate** choice, not an error — noted so Becky isn't surprised in Phase 3.
- **Kamatz katan.** Sefaria marks *kamatz katan* distinctly (e.g. כׇּל); your draft uses a
  plain *kamatz* (כָּל). A typographic/encoding choice, not a reading change. The spike
  proofs let you see how each font renders both.
